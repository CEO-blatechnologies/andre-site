Andre On Duty – Real Scenarios

Flight canceled? Andre automatically checks your calendar, confirms urgency, and suggests an alternate flight or nearby rental car agency – with one-tap booking.

Wildfire approaching? Andre warns you, reroutes your GPS, and pre-books a safe hotel — long before danger hits.

Snowed in? Andre offers lodging, notifies your boss, and checks on local road conditions. All spoken aloud, hands-free.
