Andre On Duty – Real Scenarios

Flight canceled?
Andre automatically checks your calendar, confirms urgency, and suggests nearby car rentals — with one-tap booking.

Wildfire approaching?
Andre warns you, reroutes your GPS, and pre-books a safe hotel — long before danger hits.

Snowed in?
Andre offers lodging, notifies your boss, and checks on local road conditions. All spoken aloud, hands-free.

But what if you have no home?
Andre listens through the night for sirens, voices, and weather shifts — ready to wake you, guide you, or call for help if things go south.

Andre On Duty – What Makes Him Different

Andre doesn’t just react — he anticipates.

He’s not passive like most assistants. He watches traffic, weather, and your context in real time — and steps in when you can’t afford to miss a beat.

But more than that, he defends those with no one else looking out for them.

Whether you’re on the road, on the edge, or just out of options — Andre stands watch so you don’t have to.

Because peace of mind isn’t just for people with front doors.
