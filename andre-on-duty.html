Andre On Duty

Say it once — “Andre on duty” — and rest. He’s listening, so you don’t have to.

Designed for those who sleep without walls or doors, Andre On Duty turns your device into a quiet guardian. Whether you're sleeping on the street, in a car, or in a shelter, Andre listens for nearby sounds and movement — footsteps, voices, a sudden presence — and gently alerts you with a whisper, vibration, or soft blink.

Andre doesn’t record, track, or upload anything. It works offline, burns minimal battery, and can’t be used to surveil or spy. This feature is built on trust, not intrusion. It exists for one reason only: to help people feel safe enough to finally sleep.

Activating Andre On Duty is simple: just say “Andre on duty.” He’ll quietly confirm — “I’m on duty. Rest easy.” — and begin protecting you through the night. To turn it off, say “Andre, you’re off duty,” or tap the app manually.

“This isn’t just a feature — it’s a promise. To the tired, the fearful, the forgotten. You don’t need to stay alert all night anymore. Andre’s on duty.”
