Press & Media

Andre is not your average AI. He doesn’t harvest your data, watch your every move, or pretend to be human. He’s a voice-driven companion designed to protect your time, your dignity, and your peace of mind — especially for those society often overlooks.

Created by William J. Ellsworth Jr., Andre was built with sunlight, not shadow. The project emerged from a season of loss and clarity — and from the belief that technology should work for people, not against them.
