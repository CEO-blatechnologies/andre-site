About Andre

Andre is a privacy-first digital companion designed to help you live smarter. Inspired by a promise, powered by love, and built with unwavering respect for your autonomy.

Core Features

  • Location-aware to-do assistance
  • Emergency reroute and alert features
  • Personalized reminders for life events
  • Flight cancellation and car rental support

Our Promise

Andre does not mine your data. He does not sell your attention. He simply listens, remembers, and helps—like someone who truly cares.
