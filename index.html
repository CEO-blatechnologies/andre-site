Meet Andre

Andre isn’t just another app. He’s a presence — discreet, discerning, and designed to honor your time, your privacy, and your dignity. Powered by Alexey, Andre adapts to your life without ever mining your data or selling your attention. He remembers what matters, filters what doesn’t, and helps you live on your own terms.

What He Can Do

• Voice-to-email with attachment prompts
• Real-time disaster rerouting and alerts
• Contact-based behavior learning
• Privacy-first call screening
• Location-aware reminders — no tracking
• Gift and birthday planning (if you let him)
